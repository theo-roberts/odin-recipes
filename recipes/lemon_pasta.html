lemony pastay

this lemony pasta is gonna be right up your street

ingredients

  • pasta of your choosing
  • lemon
  • garlic
  • brocc

steps

  1. get ur pasta boiling
  2. chop up some garlic
  3. zest one lemon
  4. fry the garlic in some olive oil
  5. just as the pasta is about to be ready, put the broccoli in with it
  6. once cooked drain the pasta and broccoli
  7. add the pasta and brocc to the pan, with the lemon zest
  8. mix all together and squeeze over some lemon juice
  9. enjoy!
