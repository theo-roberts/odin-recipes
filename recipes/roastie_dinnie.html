a lovely roast din

ready for a yum roast din on a cold sunday?

you've come to the right place

ingredients

  • tatoes
  • gravy
  • carrot
  • brocc
  • nice meat substitute

steps

  1. peel and chop up tatoes. boil em up
  2. when tatoes soft, drain and cook in oil in oven
  3. pop veg (carrots, brocc) in oven with some seasonings
  4. fry the meat substitute
  5. when they all cooked, make some gravy
  6. plate it all up n enjoy!
